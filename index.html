Thesis

Capital alone
is insufficient.

VC returns in life sciences and deep tech have underperformed since 2022. Life sciences deals and exits have fallen to decade lows. CRO price gouging and delays have pushed early validation outside the US.

Sifr was built to close this gap — not by writing bigger checks, but by doing the work.

01

Operate alongside inventors

Sifr partners exclusively with research labs and clinical institutions. We embed operators at the company level from day one — not as advisors, but as builders.

02

Eliminate intermediaries

In-house lab facilities execute technical and early clinical work internally — removing the CRO layer that bleeds capital and extends timelines.

03

Fail fast, iterate deliberately

Milestone-based validation before scale. Structure development to generate the right data — not more data — and prevent excessive early burn.

04

Capital follows conviction

Targeted SPVs advance validation at each milestone. No blind pools, no management fees. Capital deployed with precision against de-risked assets.
